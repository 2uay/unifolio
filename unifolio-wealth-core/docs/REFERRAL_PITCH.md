## 3. The full pitch

### The hook

If you have money in a **TFSA, RRSP, FHSA, RESP, RDSP, LIRA, or a
non-registered account** — and especially if you have more than one of
those across more than one broker — you have no idea what you actually
own. Your broker dashboards each show a slice. Spreadsheets rot. Yahoo
Finance doesn't know what an ACB is.

**Unifolio is the tracker built specifically for the Canadian who has
their money in three different places and wants one honest answer.**

---

### What it actually does (the Canadian-specific stuff)

- **Every Canadian account type, native:** TFSA, RRSP, FHSA, RESP, RDSP,
  LIRA, Margin, Cash. The math, the contribution tracking, and the tax
  treatment are all aware of which bucket each position lives in.
- **T5008-ready tax export.** ACB per ticker per account, superficial
  loss detection, 50% inclusion math, dividend / interest split, per-year
  CSVs you hand to your accountant.
- **CDR-aware portfolio.** Hold Apple as a CDR on Cboe Canada *and* as
  Apple on Nasdaq in IBKR? Unifolio treats them as one position with the
  correct FX, so your "true Apple exposure" line is actually right.
- **CAD/USD switching everywhere** with live FX. Toggle once, the entire
  app re-denominates — chart, KPI cards, donuts, tables, tax report.
- **CPI_CA benchmark.** Compare your portfolio against Canadian
  inflation, not just the S&P. (You'd be surprised who's actually
  beating CPI.)
- **Plaid Canada** for live connections — plus 50+ Canadian brokers
  documented (with screenshots) for CSV/Flex import if you don't want to
  hand over credentials.

### What's unique to Unifolio

1. **ETF X-Ray.** Click a position and Unifolio looks through every fund
   you hold and shows your real underlying exposure. The right-hand
   "True Exposure" table tells you how much of *your money* sits in
   Apple, in tech, in the US, in the top 10 names — across every ETF
   plus your direct holdings, combined. This is the feature most index
   investors didn't know they needed until they see it.

2. **Portfolio Health Score.** A single 0–100 grade with expandable
   factors: diversification, concentration, cash drag, currency mix,
   sector skew. Think "credit score, but for your portfolio."

3. **Privacy mode.** One click hides every dollar amount in the app.
   Open it on a plane, in a coffee shop, at your in-laws.

4. **Real, working real-time prices** (Finnhub feed). No 15-minute
   delay. Daily P&L actually moves.

5. **AI transaction assistant** that explains what a trade did to your
   portfolio in plain English: "this closed your TD position and
   crystallized a $1,240 gain in your non-reg account."

6. **48+ themes, including animated 'living' themes** for Pro and a
   Liquid Glass theme for Lifetime members. (Sounds silly until you

7. **One Canadian founder, weekly releases, public Discord.** When
