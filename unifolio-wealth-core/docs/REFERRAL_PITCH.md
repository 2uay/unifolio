position you bought across three lots over two years? **Nobody knows.**

That's the problem. Unifolio is the fix.

### What it actually does

**One view. Everything.**
Drop in your accounts — TFSA, RRSP, FHSA, RESP, RDSP, LIRA, non-reg,
margin, IBKR, whatever — and it stitches the whole thing together.
Holdings, cash, P&L, daily movers, sector mix, country mix, currency
mix. Reconciles transfers between accounts so the same shares don't
get double-counted.

**ETF X-Ray.** ← the part that sells itself.
Click any ETF you own. Unifolio looks INSIDE it and shows you what
you actually own. Then it does it for every ETF you hold, combined
with your direct stocks, and gives you a "True Exposure" table:
*here is your real Apple position. Here is your real tech weighting.
Here is the >8% concentration you didn't know you had.* Index
investors look at this once and go "oh."

**Tax stuff that doesn't make you cry.**
ACB tracked per ticker per account, automatically. Superficial loss
detection. Dividends split from interest. 50% inclusion math. T5008
CSV export per year — print it, attach it, done.

**CDR-aware.** Hold Apple as a CDR on Cboe Canada AND Apple on Nasdaq?
Unifolio knows it's the same company, applies the right FX, and your
"true Apple exposure" is actually right. This sounds boring until you
realize nobody else does it.

**Real-time prices.** Live Finnhub feed across every holding and your
watchlist. Not the 15-minute-delay garbage. Your daily P&L actually moves.

**CAD ↔ USD switch.** Toggle once, the entire app re-denominates —
chart, KPIs, donuts, tables, tax report. Beats Canadian inflation?
There's a CPI_CA benchmark right there to check.

**Privacy mode.** One click hides every dollar in the app. Open it on
the plane, at the coffee shop, in front of your in-laws.

**AI assistant** on your transactions. Click a trade, it tells you in
plain English what happened: *"this closed your TD position and
crystallized a $1,240 gain in your non-reg."*

**48+ themes** including animated "living" ones for Pro and a Liquid
Glass theme for Lifetime members. Sounds dumb until you spend an hour
a day in the app.
